About SmartStudentAct

A smart, collaborative platform designed to connect teachers and students through assignments, quizzes, financial planning, and rewarding achievements.

Our Vision

SmartStudentAct aims to modernize education by combining academic excellence with personal growth. Students are empowered to stay consistent with their learning, manage their budgets wisely, set achievable goals, and get data-driven insights to stay on track — all while earning rewards for their efforts.

For Teachers

  • Create and assign tasks & quizzes
  • Target students by class, grade, or school
  • Automatically receive student scores
  • Track progress over time

For Students

  • Access quizzes and assignments on dashboard
  • Answer questions with instant feedback
  • See score breakdown of correct/wrong answers
  • Stay on top of deadlines
  • Track budgets and manage personal finance
  • Set and complete weekly/monthly goals

Rewards & Growth

  • Earn points for completing assignments & goals
  • Get rewarded for consistency & achievements
  • Unlock titles like “Goal Crusher” or “Assignment Ace”
  • Motivation through performance-based rewards

AI Essay Checker

  • Get instant feedback on grammar, syntax, and style
  • Improve your writing with AI-powered suggestions
  • Check for originality and citation errors
  • Enhance clarity and flow of your essays

Real-Time Notifications & Alerts

  • Never miss a beat! Get instant pings for new assignments and quizzes.
  • Stay ahead of the curve with timely reminders for approaching deadlines.
  • Teachers can effortlessly send announcements to classes or individual students.
  • Receive alerts when your AI essay feedback is ready or when a new badge is unlocked.

Our Comprehensive Approach

SmartStudentAct doesn’t stop at academics. By blending quizzes and assignments with budget tracking, goal-setting, AI essay checking, data-driven insights, real-time notifications, and rewards, we support both the academic and personal growth of every learner. Teachers guide, students engage, and progress is rewarded.
